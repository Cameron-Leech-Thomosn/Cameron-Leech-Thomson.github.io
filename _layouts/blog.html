My Projects

Here you can find various posts about my projects; detailing what went into them, why I liked it, or why it was a pain!

These posts will usually be about a project I've either just started or just finished, including a link to their GitHub repository. The posts will be about the process of designing the project from start to finish, including certain parts that I think are interesting or were difficult to implement. Some parts that were a particular pain will get their own blog post, where I'll be able to talk about why it was so difficult and why I wanted to do it a certain way.

As well as my own projects, I may talk about things I'm currently interested in, and why I think you should be interested too! I may also provide a slightly more detailed explination of certain parts of the code, explaining why I used it and why I think it is particularly interesting or useful. Enjoy!
